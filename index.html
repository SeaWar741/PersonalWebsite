About me.

Computer Science Student

I love computers and graphic design. I’ve been working with PC’s since I was a child. I’ve always felt passion towards learning new things and decided to learn and understand more than the average people do about computers. I began formally coding in 2017 at Codeacamp with Ruby on Rails.

I’m in collage at Tecnológico de Monterrey Campus Monterrey, studying Computer Science (Ingenieria en Tecnologias Computacionales).

I’ve participated in several student groups such as Taman Keet 3933 where I worked in the programming department (Robotics Representative Team of Tecnológico de Monterrey, Campus Santa Fe), Activatec (Events Organization Group at of Tecnológico de Monterrey, Campus Santa Fe) as the group president and in the Audiovisual Production group of Tecnológico de Monterrey, Campus Santa Fe.

Right now I have a Computer Science Club called Ctrl+Alt+Tec where we teach people to solve problems, code, be creative and overall make them understand about the current technology and the ones to come.

I am currently doing alot of personal projects and research in machine learning. I specifically ❤ Python and C++ for these tasks. I geting more into big data, blockchain, image recognition, neuro science and overall cognitive computing.
